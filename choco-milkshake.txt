Making a chocolate milkshake (cold)

1. Buy dairy milk's Chocolate milkshake.
2. Take a mug filled of cold milk.
3. Add 3 tablespoon powder, from the above mentioned dairy milk product, to one mug of cold milk.
4. Pour the contents of the mug into a wider and deeper vessel and mix it with one of the BOSS mixers for about 3 minutes.
5. Your chocolate milkshake is ready serve it in the previous mug.
